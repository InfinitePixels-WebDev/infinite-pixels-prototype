How We Work

Here's Exactly How We'll Work Together

No corporate BS. Just a clear breakdown of what happens from first contact to final launch—and what you can expect at every step.

One Point of Contact, Zero Confusion

Whether we're building your website, designing graphics, managing social media, or setting up automations—you work directly with us throughout the entire process. No teams, no handoffs, just straightforward communication from start to finish.

01

Discovery Call

You reach out via email, WhatsApp, or our contact form. We respond quickly (usually same day) and schedule a call. We discuss your project needs, goals, budget, and timeline. We figure out if we're the right fit—no pressure, just honest conversation. You'll know by the end of the call if we can help.

What happens:

  • You reach out via email, WhatsApp, or our contact form
  • We respond quickly (usually same day) and schedule a call
  • We discuss your project needs, goals, budget, and timeline
  • We figure out if we're the right fit—no pressure, just honest conversation
  • You'll know by the end of the call if we can help

Deliverables:

  • Clear understanding of your project
  • Honest assessment of what we can deliver
  • Rough timeline and budget range
02

Proposal & Agreement

We send you a detailed proposal with scope, deliverables, timeline, and exact pricing. Everything is transparent—no hidden costs or vague terms. You review it and ask any questions. Once you approve, we send a simple agreement to protect both of us and schedule the project kickoff.

What happens:

  • We send you a detailed proposal with scope, deliverables, timeline, and exact pricing
  • Everything is transparent—no hidden costs or vague terms
  • You review it and ask any questions
  • Once you approve, we send a simple agreement to protect both of us
  • We schedule the project kickoff

Deliverables:

  • Detailed project proposal
  • Clear pricing breakdown
  • Simple service agreement
  • Project kickoff date
03

Design & Development

We start working on your project using the approach discussed. You get regular updates via WhatsApp or email (we don't disappear). We share work-in-progress at key milestones for your feedback. You review and tell us what needs adjustment. We keep refining until you're happy with the direction.

What happens:

  • We start working on your project using the approach discussed
  • You get regular updates via WhatsApp or email (we don't disappear)
  • We share work-in-progress at key milestones for your feedback
  • You review and tell us what needs adjustment
  • We keep refining until you're happy with the direction

Deliverables:

  • Regular progress updates
  • Work-in-progress previews
  • Opportunity to provide feedback at milestones
04

Launch & Handoff

We present the final work for your approval. You request any final changes based on the agreed revision policy. We make the final adjustments. We launch your project (website goes live, files delivered, etc.). You get all source files and access you need.

What happens:

  • We present the final work for your approval
  • You request any final changes based on the agreed revision policy
  • We make the final adjustments
  • We launch your project (website goes live, files delivered, etc.)
  • You get all source files and access you need

Deliverables:

  • Completed project launched
  • All source files and assets

What We Stand Behind

Commitments we make to every single client

On-Time Delivery Guarantee

We meet the deadline in your agreement. If we're late without valid reason, you get a discount or refund—no excuses.

Quality Satisfaction Guarantee

If the final work doesn't meet the quality standards we promised, we'll revise it until it does—or refund your money. We stand behind our work.

Clear Communication Guarantee

You'll never be left wondering what's happening. We respond within 24 hours and provide regular updates without you having to chase us.

Complete Ownership Guarantee

Every file, every asset, every line of code—it's all yours at the end. No licenses, no restrictions, no fine print. You paid for it, you own it.
