About Infinite Pixels

A New Digital Agency Built on Quality Work

We're a small team focused on delivering real results. Based in Cairo, launched in September 2024, and ready to help your business grow.

How We Started

We spent 2023 and 2024 learning and preparing. Building skills, testing ideas, and creating demos. In September 2024, we officially launched Infinite Pixels.

We're two people based in Cairo. We started with web development, then expanded as clients needed more services. Now we offer design, media production, social media management, system integration, and AI automation.

We're about a month and a half in with six services and several completed projects. We're still small, which means every client gets our full attention and best work.
